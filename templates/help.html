Help

Getting Started

To get started with BusTicketX, create an account by signing up. Once you have an account, you can log in and start booking bus tickets.

Booking Tickets

Search for available buses by entering your origin, destination, and travel date. Select a bus that suits your schedule and proceed to book your ticket by making a payment.

Managing Your Bookings

You can view and manage your bookings from your account dashboard. Here you can see your booking history, upcoming trips, and more.

Account Issues

If you encounter any issues with your account, such as trouble logging in or resetting your password, please contact our support team at support@busticketx.com.
